My Services

Whether you're navigating a life transition, caregiving stressors, the weight of past trauma, or simply feeling disconnected from yourself or others, you do not need to go through it alone.

Through individual, online therapy sessions, we'll work to broaden your awareness, strengthen your inner resources, and gently unwind the habits that no longer serve you, helping you navigate life with more clarity, calm, and vitality.

I specialize in supporting individuals experiencing:

  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Life Transitions
  • Aging
  • Pregnancy and Post-partum
  • Relationship/Attachment Issues
  • Family and/or Professional Caregiving
  • Parenting
  • Chronic illness and pain
  • PTSD and trauma
  • Grief and loss
  • Spirituality/Faith identity and questioning

My Approach

To best meet your unique needs, my approach is holistic, client-driven, strengths-based and trauma-informed.

Our work together will blend practical skill building with emotional insight and healing.

My approach integrates evidence-based models, including:

  • CBT (Cognitive Behavior Therapy)
  • ACT (Acceptance and Commitment Therapy)
  • DBT (Dialect Behavior Therapy)
  • EMDR (Eye-Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing)
  • IFS (Internal Family Systems)

We will practice skills to increase self-awareness, improve your relationship with your Self and others, and make life choices that better align with your values.

Beyond coping, we'll look at how the past influences the present and work toward more adaptive patterns that support meaningful, lasting healing.

Our work together will be:

  • Collaborative
  • Responsive to your pace and needs
  • Goal-oriented
  • Grounded in your strengths

I believe that every person has an innate capacity to heal, grow, and transform. It is never too late.

FAQs

How do I know if therapy is right for me?
I'm a firm believer that we all can benefit from greater self-awareness and more adaptive ways of navigating what life brings, to help live our lives with purpose, authenticity, and joy.

If you're navigating emotional challenges, major life transitions, caregiving stress, or feeling overwhelmed and wanting to strengthen your well-being, therapy can provide especially valuable tools and support for growth and healing.

Whether you're new to therapy or have been in therapy for years, it's natural to have questions or concerns. I offer a free 15-minute phone consultation to discuss your needs and determine if my approach aligns with your goals. Feel free to reach out!
Why Online Therapy?
Online therapy increases access to care. For those with busy lives, caregiving responsibilities, or mobility limitations, online therapy increases access to routine professional support.

Online therapy offers the convenience of meeting from a location of your choosing, reduces travel time and costs, increases flexibility in scheduling appointments, and eliminates geographical constraints.

Rest assured that attuned, connected therapeutic relationships CAN and DO occur through online therapy.

If you are new to, or at all uncomfortable with, using online health platforms, please reach out with questions. I can help walk you through it.
What can I expect during my first therapy session?
During your first session, we'll explore what brings you to therapy, clarify your goals, and address any questions you may have about the process. It's an opportunity for us to connect and see whether working together feels like a good fit.
How often should I attend therapy sessions and for how long?
The frequency of therapy sessions and length of treatment depends on individual needs and goals. During your initial consultations, we'll work together to develop a personalized treatment plan that includes a recommended session frequency based on your specific needs and circumstances.

Typically, I recommend starting with regular sessions either weekly or every other week. As your needs evolve, we can adjust the schedule—from periodic check-ins to more frequent support during challenging times.
What are your fees for service and is insurance accepted?
I am an out-of-network provider and do not bill insurance directly, in order to provide personalized, high-quality care without the constraints of insurance plans.

Individual sessions are $150 per session.

I believe in supporting access to quality care and offer a limited number of sliding-scale slots based on eligibility. I am also happy to provide superbills for those seeking out-of-network reimbursement.

Please reach out with any questions.
